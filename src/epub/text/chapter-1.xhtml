The Rasp

by

Philip MacDonald

to

The Guv’nor

All the Birds of the Air
Fell a-sighin’ and a-sobbin’
When they heard of the death
Of poor Cock Robin.
·     ·     ·     ·
“Who’ll dig his Grave?”
“I,” said the Owl,
“With my little Trowel;
I’ll dig his Grave.”
·     ·     ·     ·
“Who killed Cock Robin?”
“I,” said the Sparrow,
“With my Bow and Arrow,
I killed Cock Robin!”
